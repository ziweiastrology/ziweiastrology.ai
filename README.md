## What Is Zi Wei Dou Shu?

**Zi Wei Dou Shu** (紫微斗数), also known as **Purple Star Astrology** or **ZWDS**, is the most structurally complex system in Chinese astrology. Developed during the Song Dynasty, it maps 108+ stars across 12 life palaces to model destiny patterns with mathematical precision.

Unlike Ba Zi (八字 / Four Pillars of Destiny), Western astrology, I Ching, or Tarot, Zi Wei Dou Shu offers:

- **12 Palace System** — Career, Wealth, Relationships, Health, Property, and more
- **108+ Star Catalog** — each star with brightness states, interactions, and transformations
- **Decade + Annual Overlays** — predictive timing at 10-year, yearly, monthly, and daily resolution
- **Mathematical Determinism** — no subjective "interpretation," pure algorithmic mapping
